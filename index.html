How to Effectively Record Your Trades:

  • Track your emotions: It helps to know how psychological factors affect your trades.
  • Record your trading setup in detail: Identify patterns in setups for more successful trades.
  • Include your entry and exit points: Understand your strategy and outcomes better.
  • Set goals for each trade: Keep track of your targets to stay focused on your objectives.
  • Review and analyze past trades: Continuous learning from your mistakes is key to improvement.
